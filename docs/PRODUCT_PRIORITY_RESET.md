# Product Priority Reset — Arkansas Everywhere

**Effective:** Pass 31 onward  
**North star:** Best Arkansas community calendar → event database → knowledge graph → civic intelligence engine. Campaign tools sit **on top**, not underneath.

## Stop (until calendar density targets are met)

- Campaign feature expansion
- Paid feature expansion
- CRM / persuasion tooling
- Public exposure of AI scoring, opportunity rankings, relationship density, or private campaign intelligence

## Build (next several passes)

- **Density** — events per county, per city, per lane
- **Content** — sourced facts, not invented lore
- **Drill-down pages** — rich entity profiles, not thin listings
- **Lanes** — the 10-lane build program (see `data/event-lanes/master-calendar-lane-map.json`)
- **Narratives** — About, History, Timeline, FAQs for AI retrieval SEO
- **Internal links** — city ↔ county ↔ org ↔ event ↔ festival
- **AI knowledge** — indexes, research tasks, narrative scaffolds
